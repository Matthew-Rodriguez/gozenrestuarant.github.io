About Gozen

An Experience For All

Since opening our doors in Visalia in May 2006, Gozen Japaneese Restaurant has become the premiere destination for quality sushi and teppanyaki in the South Valley.

If it's a night out with friends, celebrating a birthday, or just hungry for fresh food, Gozen's is the place to be!

The meaning of Gozen is “Royal Dining”. Our restaurant begins every day with the freshest fish and finest ingredients available, ensuring that our guests can expect the best possible cuisine and service.
